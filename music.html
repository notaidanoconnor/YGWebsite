Yung Grandma MUSIC

The 10-Album Project

I dropped ten albums in one day because I was tired of waiting around for my music to be good enough to release. I never considered any of my work to be finished, and that held me back for a long time. I have since realized that it doesn't matter if all of your music is good. I was lost in discovering my sound, and by releasing such a wide variety of styles and genres of music, I left it to the listener to show me what they like and what they don't. Many of the dedicated fans of Yung Grandma had also long awaited an official release, listening through audio files and old posts on SoundCloud in the meantime. Maybe Yung Grandma won't be a project forever, but these ten albums are my first steps in understanding how I'll make my name in the world of music. YNGGMA4L >:)
